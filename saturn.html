Saturn is the 6th planet from the Sun and is a gas giant without a solid surface. Its atmosphere primarily contains hyrdogen and helium. Although these conditions will not support life on Saturn, some of its many moons (82) may have conditions to support life. Pioneer 11, Voyager 1, and Voyager 2 all flew by Saturn, but Cassini orbited Saturn 294 times from 2004-2017. Saturn is known for its rings, likely made of comets, asteroids, or pieces of moons, as well as chunks of ice and rocks coated in dust.
