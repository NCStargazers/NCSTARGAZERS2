Welcome NCStargazers!

NCStargazers is an online community to share astronomy information, recent space news, and promote local astrophysics events! This organization also has a non-profit partnership with FillingInTheBlanks to inspire astronomy within other local groups in CT!

Please DM on insta with any questions: local info, curiosities, any info I should add, viewings you would like to share, any website bugs. Thank you!
